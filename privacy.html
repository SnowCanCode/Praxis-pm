Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Praxis™, we collect the following information:

Account Information:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Password (encrypted)

Usage Data:

  • Projects and tasks you create
  • Files you upload
  • Team collaboration data
  • Activity logs and timestamps

Technical Data:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the Praxis service
  • Process your transactions and subscriptions
  • Send important service updates
  • Respond to support requests
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Firebase for authentication and data storage, which complies with international security standards.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data only in these circumstances:

  • With your team members: Project and task data is shared with collaborators you invite
  • Service providers: We use third-party services (Firebase, payment processors) that may access your data to provide their services
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us through our Discord community.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Praxis uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site usage

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

Praxis is intended for users 13 years and older. Users under 18 require parental consent. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. After account deletion, we may retain certain data for legal or operational purposes.

9. International Users

Praxis is operated from Canada. If you access the service from outside Canada, your data may be transferred to and processed in Canada.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through the Service.

For privacy questions or concerns, join our Discord community.
